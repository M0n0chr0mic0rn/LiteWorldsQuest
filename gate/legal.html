Kotia, Litecoin & On-Chain Assets

LiteWorlds utilizes native on-chain assets such as Litecoin and Kotia as fundamental units of interaction. Kotia operates on its own independent blockchain with a protocol-defined supply model. No official maximum supply is documented at this time.

Tokens created using this infrastructure are on-chain inscriptions, which are indexed and retrieved using open indexers compatible with OmniLite or Ordinals on the Litecoin blockchain. A custom inscription method using OP_RETURN is planned for Kotia to support a native token protocol tailored to the ecosystem. These tokens represent structured metadata anchored on-chain and are not intended as monetary units.

Custodial Wallet (Experimental Mode)

The current wallet feature exists solely to enable temporary access to infrastructure services. It is not intended for long-term asset storage or as a secure value repository. A non-custodial version is in development to grant users full control of their keys and assets.

DEX Integration

Any interaction with decentralized exchanges (DEX) occurs through public smart contracts. LiteWorlds does not operate trading services but may offer sample interfaces and development examples. The “Trader Bot” exists purely for demonstration purposes.
